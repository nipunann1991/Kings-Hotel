About Kings Hotel

Offering perfect, high quality and comfortable accommodation in a purpose built hotel, Kings Hotel is a boutique type property set in the heart of Nuwaraeliya the beautiful, misty hill city and 'The Little England' in Sri Lanka.

Perfect for both business and pleasure visits to this vibrant city to enjoy a fabulous vacation. Kings Hotel is within easy reach of many of the most important sceneries and attractions are minutes away.
